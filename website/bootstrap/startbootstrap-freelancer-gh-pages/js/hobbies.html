AIR CHAIR

IN ACTION


Q&A

  1. What is it?
    • The air chair is a watersport where the ridcer gets towed behind either a boat or a wave runner
    • The air chair is a seat positioned on top of a thick ski with a long foil underneath
    • The foil allows the rider to glide through waves and move up and down.

  2. Where do you do it?
    • The air chair can be used in any body of water
    • My favorite place is Lake powell

  3. Is it fun?
    • The adrenaline rush one receives from the air chair is unmatched by anything else
